Bachata Sensual meets Kizomba on the beach

Get ready for 6 days of Bachata Sensual and Kizomba in a city blessed with one of the mose beautiful beaches in Europe: Cádiz. You can choose to have 12 hours of classes of Bachata Sensual, 12 hours of classes of Kizomba or 24 hours of Kizomba. There will be parties every night and the beach is only a block away from the hotel. It will be a week to never forget!

Learn from the best

Top international instructors.


Korke and Judith
Bachata Sensual

Korke started developing his unique Bachata Sensual style in Cádiz 10 years ago. Together with his partner Judith, he travels around the world teaching at festivals and judging the world's most important bachata contests.

Chaves and Silvia
Bachata Sensual

Chaves and Silvia are close friends of Korke and Judith and they have worked together with them to create and define this special dance style that is Bachata Sensual. They have achieved the BachataStars Europe title and teach workshops all over the world.

Albir and Carola
Kizomba

Albir has been an important player in the Kizomba revolution in Europe. Together with Carola, he will be teaching all the secrets of Kizomba, Tarraxinha and Semba.

Ouzin Gozmir
Kizomba

Ouzin is one of the main teachers of Kizomba in Portugal. He will be sharing with us all his feeling and flow.

The amazing venue

All the workshops will happen in the four star Hotel Monte Puertatierra . Only one block from the beach, this hotel gives a fantastic price of 50 Euros per night to festival participants, including free Wi-Fi and breakfast.
