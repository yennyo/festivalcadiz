Bachata Sensual in Cádiz

After five successful editions, get ready for Sensual Week 2019! It will be a splendid week of Bachata Sensual in Cádiz, a city with a rich history that is blessed with some of the most beautiful beaches in Europe.

Every track consists of 10 hours of classes of Bachata Sensual especially optimized for you. There is also a show team track, and a number of 5-hour additional tracks. There will be epic parties every night from Monday until Saturday, and the beach is only a block away!

As every year, it will be a fantastic week to never forget!

Organizers

This unique festival is brought to you by Korke, Judith, Yenny, and Andreas.

Over the last couple of years, they have been carefully fine-tuning the festival's formula, so as to give all participants a quality dance event, unique in the world.

Workshops, beach, and parties!

The workshops will be in hotel Occidental Cádiz (formerly known as hotel Barceló) and the Colegio Salesianos, only one block away from the beach: in order to ensure a quality event, maximum 50 people are allowed per track.

Cádiz is famous for its endless white beaches. There will be drinks and food for purchase in the "chiringuitos" or local beach kiosks.

Every night there will be a dedicated party with the best music in different venues throughout the city!

There are also AirBnB apartments and other hotels in the area.
