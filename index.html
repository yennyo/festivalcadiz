Bachata Sensual meets Kizomba in the beach

Get ready for 6 days of Bachata Sensual and Kizomba in a city blessed with one of the mose beautiful beaches in Europe: Cadiz. You can choose to have 12 hours classes of Bachata Sensual, 12 hours of classes of Kizomba or 24 hours of Kizomba. There will be parties every night and the beach is only a block away from the hotel..... it will be a week to never forget!


Learn from the best

Top international instructors.


Korke and Judith
Bachata Sensual

Korke made the Bachata Sensual dance in Cadiz 10 years ago. Together with Judith, they travel around the world teaching in festivals and being the jury of the most important Bachata contests in the world.

Chaves and Silvia
Bachata Sensual

Chaves and Silvia are close friends from Korke and Judith and they have worked together to create and define this new dance style. They have achieved the BachataStars Europe title and have the most amazing tricks and shows.

Albir and Carola
Kizomba

Albir has been part of the Kizomba revolution in Europe. Together with Carola, they are going to teach all the secrets of Kizomba, Tarraxinha and Semba.

Ouzin Gozmir
Kizomba

Ouzin is one of the main teachers of Kizomba in Portugal, he will be sharing with us all his feeling and flow.

The amazing venue

All the workshops will happen in the 4 stars hotel Hotel Monte Puertatierra . Only one block from the beach, this hotel gives a fantastic price to our participants of 50 Euros per night, including free wifi and breakfast.
