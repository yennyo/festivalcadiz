Bachata Sensual meets Kizomba on the beach

Get ready for 5 days of Bachata Sensual and Kizomba in a city blessed with one of the most beautiful beaches in Europe: Cádiz. You can choose to have 12 hours of classes of Bachata Sensual, 12 hours of classes of Kizomba or 24 hours of both. There will be parties every night and the beach is only a block away from the hotel. It will be a week to never forget!


Learn from the best

Top international instructors


Korke and Judith
Bachata Sensual

Korke has been teaching dance for 14 years and started developing his unique Bachata Sensual style in Cádiz 10 years ago. Together with his partner Judith, he travels around the world teaching at festivals and judging the world's most important bachata contests.

Chaves and Silvia
Bachata Sensual

Chaves and Silvia are close friends of Korke and Judith and they have worked together with them to create and define this special dance style that is Bachata Sensual. They have achieved the BachataStars Europe title and teach workshops all over the world.

Albir and Carola
Kizomba

Albir has been an important driver of the Kizomba revolution in Europe. Together with Carola, he will be teaching all the secrets of Kizomba, Tarraxinha and Semba.

Ouzin Gozmir
Kizomba

Ouzin has danced as a professional for 9 years. He comes from Morocco and he has been teaching African dances for 8 years in Lisbon. He will be sharing with us all his feeling and flow.
