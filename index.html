Bachata Sensual in Cádiz

After three succesful editions, get ready for another splendid week of Bachata Sensual in Cádiz, a city with a rich history that is blessed with some of the most beautiful beaches in Europe.

You can choose to have 10 hours of classes of Bachata Sensual in intermediate, advanced, and pro level. There is also a show team track, and two 5-hour lady styling and new talent tracks. There will be epic parties every night from Monday until Saturday, and the beach is only a block away.

As every year, it will be a fantastic week to never forget!

Organizers

This unique festival is brought to you by Korke, Judith, Yenny, and Andreas.

Over the last couple of years, they have been carefully fine-tuning the festival's formula, so as to give all participants a quality dance event, unique in the world.

Workshops, beach, and parties!

The workshops will be in hotel Occidental Cádiz (formerly known as hotel Barceló) and the Raul Calvo gym, only one block away from the beach: in order to ensure a quality event, maximum 25 couples are allowed per track.

Cádiz is famous for its endless white beaches. There will be drinks and food for purchase in the "chiringuitos" or local beach kiosks.

Every night there will be a dedicated party with the best music in different venues throughout the city. This year, we'll end the week in style with a closing party on Saturday!
