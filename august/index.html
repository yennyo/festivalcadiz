The application process

You apply by filling out a form and submitting a video of yourself dancing a Bachata Sensual improvisation with a partner.

Things to keep in mind:

  • make sure your video is at least 60 seconds long
  • it should be an improvisation with leading and following, not a choreography
  • we only accept links from YouTube, Instagram, Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox
