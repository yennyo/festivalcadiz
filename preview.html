Bachata Sensual in Cádiz

After successful 2014 & 2015 editions, get ready for another splendid five six days of Bachata Sensual in Cádiz, a city with a rich history that is blessed with some of the most beautiful beaches in Europe.

You can choose to have 10 hours of classes of Bachata Sensual in intermediate, advanced, or pro level. There will be epic parties every night and the beach is only a block away. It will be a fantastic week to never forget!



Learn from the best

Top international instructors


Korke & Judith
Bachata Sensual

Korke has been teaching dance for 17 years and started developing his unique Bachata Sensual style in Cádiz around 15 years ago. Together with his beautiful partner Judith, he travels around the world teaching at various dance festivals and judging the world's most important bachata contests.

Chaves & Silvia
Bachata Sensual

Chaves and Silvia are close friends of Korke and Judith and they have worked together with them to create and define the special dance style that is Bachata Sensual. They are also members of the Island Touch bachata family, and teach workshops all over the world.

Ouzin & Ariella
Show team

Ouzin and his partner Ariella are based in Lisbon, where they teach various African dances, including kizomba and semba. Just like last year, they will be sharing with us all their feeling and flow, in a special show team track. Be ready to be surprised!

Luis & Andrea
Bachata Sensual

Luis & Andrea have been taking the bachata world by storm throughout the last year. They've been teaching all over Europe, China, and Latin America, and are known for their elegant moves, razor-sharp execution, and great musicality.

Kike & Fanía
Bachata Sensual

Kike & Fanía started dancing together in 2011, when they won the title of international champions of Bachatarte. In 2012, they also became sub-champions of Bachata Stars Europe. They've been teaching at festivals all over Spain, Europe and beyond, and are known for their great connection and strong musical interpretation.

Pablo & Raquel
Bachata Sensual

Pablo & Raquel have been dancing together since 2014 and they've been champions in several bachata competitions. In the last two years, they've been teaching at many festivals throughout Europe, and they will bring their technique and style to the Sensual Week workshops as well!

Workshops, beach, and parties!

The workshops will be in hotel Barceló, only one block away from the beach: in order to ensure a quality event, a maximum of 25 couples is allowed per track.

Cádiz is famous for its endless white beaches. There will be drinks and food for purchase in the "chiringuitos" or local beach kiosks.

Every night there will be a dedicated party with the best music in different venues throughout the city. Be ready to dance like you've never danced before!
