Bachata Sensual in Cádiz

After successful 2014 & 2015 editions, get ready for another splendid five six days of Bachata Sensual in Cádiz, a city with a rich history that is blessed with some of the most beautiful beaches in Europe.

You can choose to have 10 hours of classes of Bachata Sensual in intermediate, advanced, or pro level. There will be epic parties every night from Monday until Saturday and the beach is only a block away. It will be a fantastic week to never forget!

Workshops, beach, and parties!

The workshops will be in hotel Barceló and the Raul Calvo gym, only one block away from the beach: in order to ensure a quality event, maximum 25 couples are allowed per track.

Cádiz is famous for its endless white beaches. There will be drinks and food for purchase in the "chiringuitos" or local beach kiosks.

Every night there will be a dedicated party with the best music in different venues throughout the city. This year, we'll end the week with a bang with a big party on Saturday!
