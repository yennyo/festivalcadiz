Bachata Sensual meets Kizomba on the beach

After a successful 2014 edition, get ready for another splendid five days of Bachata Sensual and Kizomba in Cádiz, a city blessed with one of the most beautiful beaches in Europe.

You can choose to have 10 hours of classes of Bachata Sensual (intermediate or advanced level) or 10 hours of classes of Kizomba. There will be epic parties every night and the beach is only a block away. It will be a fantastic week to never forget!



Learn from the best

Top international instructors


Korke & Judith
Bachata Sensual

Korke has been teaching dance for 14 years and started developing his unique Bachata Sensual style in Cádiz 10 years ago. Together with his partner Judith, he travels around the world teaching at festivals and judging the world's most important bachata contests.

Chaves & Silvia
Bachata Sensual

Chaves and Silvia are close friends of Korke and Judith and they have worked together with them to create and define this special dance style that is Bachata Sensual. They have achieved the BachataStars Europe title and teach workshops all over the world.

Enah & Carolina
Kizomba

Enah, from France, and Carolina, from Spain, dance what they call "Urban Kiz". Not putting aside kizomba's Afro-Cape Verdian roots they leave their mark with a creative mix of various dance influences.

Morenasso & Anaïs
Kizomba

Morenasso, from Angola, and his partner Anais Millon, from Martinique, are world famous kizomba artists and known for the sensuality, creativity and dynamism they bring to their performances and workshops.

Ouzin & Ariella
Show team

Ouzin and his partner Ariella are based in Lisbon, where they teach various African dances, including kizomba and semba. They will be sharing with us all their feeling and flow, in a special show team track.

Workshops, beach, and parties!

The workshops will be in hotel Barceló, only one block away from the beach: in order to ensure a quality event, a maximum of 50 people is allowed per track.

Cádiz is famous for its endless white beaches. There will be bachata and kizomba music, as well as drinks and food in one of the "chiringuitos" or local beach kiosks.

Every night there will be a dedicated party with the best bachata, kizomba and salsa music in different venues throughout the city. Be ready to dance like you've never danced before!
